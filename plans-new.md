# GeoScale Plans - Final Structure

## Single Site Plans

### 1. Starter - £49 one-off
Perfect for small businesses who just need their geo pages created once.

**Includes:**
- 1 website
- 50 geo-landing pages
- GPT content generation
- WordPress publishing
- Meta titles and descriptions
- Testimonial integration
- Related keyword inclusion
- CSV import for locations

**Does not include:**
- Rank tracking
- Ongoing optimisations

---

### 2. Pro - £29/month
Ideal for website owners who want active monitoring and ongoing improvements.

**Includes:**
- 1 website
- 400 combination pages
- Up to 50 keywords tracked
- Every-other-day rank updates
- Keyword performance insights
- Bulk meta editor
- Content refresh on demand

---

## Agency Plans

### 3. Agency - £99/month
Built for smaller agencies managing multiple local clients.

**Includes:**
- Up to 5 client websites
- 400 pages per website
- 50 tracked keywords per site (250 total)
- Every-other-day rank tracking
- Client dashboards
- WordPress plugin for all sites
- Bulk meta optimisation

**Add-ons:**
- Extra sites: £20/site/month (includes 50 more tracked keywords)

---

### 4. Agency Plus - £199/month
For growing or established agencies with many client websites.

**Includes:**

- 50 tracked keywords per site (500 total)
- Every-other-day rank tracking
- White-label client access
- Agency-level reporting
- All bulk editing tools
- WordPress plugin for all sites
- Priority support

**Add-ons:**
- Extra sites: £20/site/month (includes 50 more tracked keywords)
