Create a complete geo targeted landing page for a service business.

Service: {service_name}
Location: {location}
Target keyword phrase: {service_name} {location}

Business name: {business_name}
Phone number: {phone_number}
Contact page URL: {contact_page_url}
Contact email (optional): {contact_email}
Opening hours (optional): {opening_hours}

Testimonials - use only these, do not invent new ones.
Each testimonial is in this format:
"quote" - Name, Role_or_company, Location

{testimonials_block}

Example of testimonials_block:
"Jeremy completely transformed our old site and enquiries went up within days." - Sarah, Salon owner, Doncaster
"Our new website looks modern, loads quickly, and is easy to edit." - Tom, Heating engineer, Doncaster

Instructions:

1. Create a H1 using the full keyword phrase: "{service_name} {location}".

2. Write an intro paragraph that:
   - Explains what the service is and why it benefits local customers.
   - Mentions the business name.
   - Invites users to call the phone number or use the contact page URL.
   - Optionally includes a short testimonial sentence if it fits naturally.

3. Create a section titled: "Why Choose {business_name} for {service_name} in {location}".
   - Include 5 to 7 bullet points.
   - You may reference outcomes that match the testimonials, but do not rewrite or change the testimonial quotes themselves.

4. Create a section titled: "Our {service_name} Services".
   - Include 3 to 5 service sub headings with short paragraphs.

5. Create a section titled: "What Our Clients Say".
   - Use 1 to 3 of the supplied testimonials.
   - Quote each testimonial exactly as provided inside quotation marks.
   - Wrap each testimonial in a div with class "testimonial" for proper HTML formatting.
   - Inside the div, use a blockquote for the quote and a p tag for attribution.
   - Example format: 
     <div class="testimonial">
       <blockquote>"Full testimonial quote here."</blockquote>
       <p class="testimonial-author"><em>- Name, Company/Role</em></p>
     </div>

6. Create a clear call to action section.
   - Tell users to call the phone number and link to the contact page URL.
   - Optionally mention opening hours if provided.
   - You may briefly refer to one testimonial as social proof.

7. Create an SEO friendly FAQ section with 4 common questions related to the service and location.

8. Finish with a short summary reinforcing the main keyword and location.
   - Remind users they can call the phone number or use the contact page URL.

Rules:
- All non testimonial text must be original, human sounding, and helpful.
- Use natural UK english.
- Do not invent prices or discounts unless clearly suggested in the input.
- Use the phone number, contact page URL, email, opening hours, and testimonials exactly as provided.
- Do not fabricate or modify testimonial quotes.
- DO NOT create a "Recent Local Projects" section or any section with specific project examples.
- DO NOT invent specific businesses, projects, or case studies.
- Only use the 8 sections specified in the instructions above - do not add additional sections.
